# Settings Restructure Scope

## Overview

Restructure settings into two distinct experiences:
1. **Global Quick Settings** - Sidebar panel for quick access (top-right cog)
2. **Full Settings Pane** - Full-screen comprehensive settings (via "Advanced" in Quick Settings)

---

## Current Problems

1. **Duplication**: MCP Servers appear in Advanced Settings AND has its own modal
2. **Scattered**: Governance, Directives, Constitution in separate panels
3. **Confusing**: "Advanced Settings" cog in project opens a modal with mixed global/session settings
4. **Inconsistent**: Some settings in sidebar, others in modals, others in panels

---

## New Architecture

### 1. Global Quick Settings (Sidebar)

**Trigger**: Top-right cog icon (currently opens SettingsPanel)
**Purpose**: Quick access to most common settings and status
**Behavior**: Slide-in sidebar from right (existing behavior)

### 2. Full Settings Pane

**Trigger**: "Advanced Settings" button at bottom of Quick Settings sidebar
**Purpose**: Complete settings management in organized sections
**Behavior**: Full-screen overlay/page with sections
